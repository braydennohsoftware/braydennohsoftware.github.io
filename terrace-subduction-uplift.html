Terrace Subduction Uplift

This simulator models terrace formation from subduction zone earthquake cycles. A curved megathrust fault accumulates interseismic slip deficit and releases it as coseismic uplift, while stream-power erosion carves the topography between events. Press Play and tune the sliders.
